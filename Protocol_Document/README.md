This document contains the protocol developed and followed for the study “Exploring LLM-Supported Architectural Reasoning for Context-Aware and Self-Adaptive Software Systems”. The protocol specifies the review objectives, research questions, search strategy, digital libraries, search strings, inclusion and exclusion criteria, quality assessment criteria, data extraction items, and procedures for study selection, validation (including the Quasi Gold Standard), and snowballing. It serves as a methodological blueprint for the review and ensures transparency, rigor, and replicability of the study. The protocol was defined prior to executing the review and guided all subsequent search, screening, quality assessment, and analysis activities reported in the article and replication package.
