These Excel sheets contain the complete empirical data used in the systematic literature review reported in the paper “Exploring LLM‑Supported Architectural Reasoning for Context‑Aware and Self‑Adaptive Software Systems”. The spreadsheets document all phases of the review process, including: 1-Automated search results from ACM Digital Library, IEEE Xplore, and Scopus 2-Duplicate identification and removal records 3-Screening decisions based on defined inclusion and exclusion criteria 4-Primary study selection, including reasons for inclusion 5-Data extraction for primary studies, covering items D1–D18 (e.g., LLM models, architectural activities, lifecycle phases, optimization techniques, evaluation methods, trends, gaps, and future work) 6-Quality assessment scores for included studies 7-Backward and forward snowballing results 8-Extended evidence base of architecture‑focused LLM studies used for scope contextualization

Together, these Excel sheets provide a transparent, traceable, and reproducible record of all decisions and analyses underlying the study. They enable researchers to replicate the review, verify results, or extend the analysis to additional domains or research questions.
