These Excel sheets contain the complete empirical data used in the systematic literature review reported in the paper “Large Language Models for Software Architecture Design Support in Self-Adaptive Systems: Early Insights from an Exploratory Systematic Review”. The spreadsheets document all phases of the review process, including: 1-Automated search results from ACM Digital Library, IEEE Xplore, and Scopus.
Duplicate identification and removal records
Screening decisions based on defined inclusion and exclusion criteria
Primary study selection, including reasons for inclusion
Data extraction for primary studies, covering items D1–D18 (e.g., LLM models, architectural activities, lifecycle phases, optimization techniques, evaluation methods, trends, gaps, and future work)
Quality assessment scores for included studies 
Backward and forward snowballing results
Extended evidence base of architecture‑focused LLM studies used for scope contextualization
Together, these Excel sheets provide a transparent, traceable, and reproducible record of all decisions and analyses underlying the study. They enable researchers to replicate the review, verify results, or extend the analysis to additional domains or research questions
